FAQ's

Ready Mix Plaster is a factory-made, pre-mixed plastering solution that requires only water to be added on site. It ensures consistent quality, faster application, and eliminates the need for on-site mixing of sand and cement.

Tile adhesive provides superior bonding strength, reduces tile breakage, eliminates the need for soaking tiles, and ensures faster installation compared to traditional cement mortar methods.

Wall putty is specifically designed to provide a smooth, even surface for paint application. Unlike regular cement, it offers better adhesion, crack resistance, and creates a perfect base for both interior and exterior painting.

Block adhesive should be mixed with water to achieve a workable consistency. Apply it evenly on block surfaces using a trowel, then press blocks firmly together. It sets quickly and provides strong bonding for AAC blocks and other masonry units.
