Wall Care Putty

Smooth, Durable & Paint-Ready Surfaces

Our Wall Care Putty is a premium, white cement–based finishing material formulated to create a smooth, strong and paint-ready surface. It eliminates undulations, covers minor imperfections, and improves the longevity and finish of paint on interior and exterior walls.

Features & Benefits

  • Ultra-smooth, premium white finish
  • Enhances paint durability and appearance
  • Excellent adhesion on plastered and concrete surfaces
  • Crack-resistant and water-resistant formulation
  • Ideal for interior & exterior wall preparation
  • Prevents flaking, damp patches and uneven paint finish
  • Easy to mix, apply and sand

Ideal For

  • Interior wall finishing
  • Exterior wall smoothening
  • Residential, commercial & institutional buildings
  • Pre-paint surface preparation
