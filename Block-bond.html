BLOCK BOND

High-Strength Adhesive for AAC & Concrete Blocks

BLOCK BOND is a polymer-modified, ready-to-use block joining adhesive designed for fixing AAC blocks, concrete blocks, fly-ash bricks and lightweight blocks. It replaces traditional sand–cement mortar, offering superior adhesion, faster construction and lower material usage.

Features & Benefits:

Strong bonding strength for all types of blocks

Thin-joint technology reduces material consumption

Faster wall construction with better accuracy

Crack-resistant and shrinkage-controlled formula

Consistent quality with no on-site mixing errors

Lightweight and easy to apply with a notched trowel

Improved wall alignment and structural stability

Ideal For:

  • AAC block masonry
  • Lightweight concrete block walls
  • Fly-ash brick construction
  • Load-bearing and non-load-bearing walls
  • Residential, commercial & industrial projects
