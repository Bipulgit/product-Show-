General Questions

Waterproofing is the process of making a structure resistant to water penetration. It's crucial for protecting buildings from water damage, preventing structural deterioration, mold growth, and maintaining the longevity of the construction.

The lifespan of waterproofing depends on the quality of materials used and proper application. Our premium waterproofing solutions typically last 10-15 years or more with proper maintenance.

Waterproofing should ideally be applied during the construction phase. However, it can also be applied to existing structures during dry seasons when temperatures are moderate (between 10°C to 35°C).
