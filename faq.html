Everything you need to know about PLASTOPROOF plaster solutions

Plastoproof is an innovative construction-materials brand offering next-generation, eco-friendly, and highly durable solutions such as Ready-Mix Plaster Mortar, Tile Adhesives, Block Bond, and Wall Care Putty. Developed by experienced engineers and industry experts, Plastoproof focuses on delivering high-performance, cost-effective products designed to improve construction efficiency, strength, and overall project quality.

Plastoproof Ready-Mix Plaster is a patented, premixed, graded-granule, polymer-based cement mortar that eliminates the uncertainties of on-site mixing. Unlike conventional sand-cement plaster, it offers:

  • Consistent quality due to factory-controlled grading
  • 30% more coverage
  • 50% labor cost savings
  • Waterproof and crack-resistant performance
  • Self-curing properties (up to 70% reduced water requirement)
  • Smooth, durable finish and stronger adhesion

This leads to faster execution, reduced wastage, and long-term durability for all types of walls and surfaces.

Plastoproof offers two variants tailored to specific construction needs:

  • Pro (Type B): Suitable for general construction — brick walls, block walls, AAC blocks, concrete surfaces, room interiors, etc.
  • Pro Plus (Type A): Designed for high-moisture and critical areas like bathrooms, kitchens, exterior walls, or areas requiring enhanced waterproofing and durability.
